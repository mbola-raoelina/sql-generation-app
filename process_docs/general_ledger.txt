PROCESS: General Ledger Accounting
OVERVIEW: This process covers financial reporting and period close activities in Oracle Fusion Financials.

STEPS:
1. Journal Entry
   - Tables: GL_JE_HEADERS, GL_JE_LINES, GL_JE_BATCHES
   - Columns: JE_HEADER_ID, JE_LINE_NUM, LEDGER_ID, PERIOD_NAME, ACCOUNTED_DR, ACCOUNTED_CR
   - Business Rules: Journals must balance (debit = credit) before posting

2. Subledger Accounting
   - Tables: XLA_AE_HEADERS, XLA_AE_LINES, XLA_TRANSACTION_ENTITIES
   - Columns: AE_HEADER_ID, APPLICATION_ID, ENTITY_ID, ACCOUNTING_DATE
   - Business Rules: Subledger journal entries must be transferred to GL

3. Period Close
   - Tables: GL_PERIODS, GL_JE_HEADERS, GL_JE_LINES
   - Columns: PERIOD_NAME, START_DATE, END_DATE, CLOSING_STATUS
   - Business Rules: Prior periods must be closed before current period processing

4. Financial Reporting
   - Tables: GL_BALANCES, GL_CODE_COMBINATIONS, GL_LEDGERS
   - Columns: PERIOD_NAME, CURRENCY_CODE, ACTUAL_FLAG, PERIOD_NET_DR, PERIOD_NET_CR
   - Business Rules: Balances must be translated for reporting currencies

5. Reconciliation
   - Tables: GL_RECONCILIATIONS, GL_RECONCILIATION_ITEMS
   - Columns: RECONCILIATION_ID, STATUS, RECONCILIATION_DATE
   - Business Rules: Account reconciliations must be completed before period close

KEY RELATIONSHIPS:
- GL_JE_HEADERS.JE_HEADER_ID → GL_JE_LINES.JE_HEADER_ID
- GL_CODE_COMBINATIONS.CODE_COMBINATION_ID → GL_JE_LINES.CODE_COMBINATION_ID
- GL_CODE_COMBINATIONS.CODE_COMBINATION_ID → GL_BALANCES.CODE_COMBINATION_ID

COMMON QUERIES:
- Find unposted journal entries
- Identify unbalanced journals
- Track period close status
- Report on account balances
